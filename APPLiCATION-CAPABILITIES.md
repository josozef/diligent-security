Here’s a concise, product‑by‑product view of the **Diligent Risk portfolio** and what each product can do.

---

### 1. Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) suite

#### AI Risk Essentials (aka *Risk Essentials*)
Lightweight ERM for low‑maturity or spreadsheet‑driven programs.

Key capabilities:
- **AI risk discovery & benchmarking** from 120k–180k+ risks in SEC 10‑K filings (filter by company, industry, category).  
- **Three‑step workflow**:  
  - Identify risks (AI‑suggested + manual).  
  - Assess (5‑point impact/likelihood, auto‑calculated scores).  
  - Mitigate (simple mitigation plans per risk).  
- **Interactive heatmap & overview dashboards** for a basic, board‑ready view of risk posture.  
- **Fast deployment** (< 7 days) with minimal configuration, aimed at GCs, lone risk managers, and small teams.  

Designed as a “starter” tier, with a **seamless upgrade path** into ERM Risk Pro / Plus (same backend).

---

#### Diligent ERM (Risk Pro / Risk Plus)
Full‑featured ERM for mature programs, built mainly on **Risk Manager, Strategy, Frameworks, Projects, and Activity Center** apps.

Core capabilities:
- **Centralized risk register & controls**  
  - Single source of truth for strategic & operational risks, controls, and risk events.  
  - Support for both **top‑down (enterprise/strategic)** and **bottom‑up (operational) risk** models.  

- **AI‑powered risk identification & benchmarking**  
  - AI Risk Discovery (internal) plus **Moody’s ERM external data** (Risk Pro/Plus add‑ons) for benchmarking against peers across financial, cyber, supplier risk, etc.  

- **Configurable assessments & workflows**  
  - Custom scoring models, dimensions, and appetite; scheduled assessments and recurring workflows.  
  - Risk‑to‑control mapping and links into assurance or audit work (Internal Audit / Projects).  

- **Continuous monitoring & control testing (higher tiers)**  
  - Control library, automated/assisted control testing, and continuous monitoring capabilities via frameworks, Projects and analytics tooling.  

- **Analytics, dashboards & board reporting**  
  - Near real‑time dashboards via **Activity Center** (Power BI‑style) for heatmaps, trend lines, KRIs, and entity‑level views.  
  - **Diligent ERM Reporting, powered by Moody’s** for external vs. internal risk views and templated **board‑ready ERM packs**.  
  - Native integration with **Diligent Boards** to publish dashboards and narrative reports directly into the board portal.  

- **Security & scale**  
  - FedRAMP and DoD IL5 authorization options, ISO 27001, and multi‑entity/global support for complex orgs.  

---

### 2. IT & Cyber Risk Management

Diligent’s IT Risk portfolio is packaged as **IT Compliance**, **IT Risk Management**, and **IT Vendor Risk Management**, typically in Essentials / Pro / Plus tiers.

#### IT Compliance
- **Frameworks & regulations**: Map and manage obligations for NIST, ISO 27001, SOC2, NIS2, DORA and other security/compliance frameworks.  
- **Control library & testing**: Define controls, run assessments, manage evidence, handle exceptions, and support certification/audit readiness.  
- **Workflow automation** for assessments, attestations, and remediation, with dashboards for compliance status.  

#### IT Risk Management (ITRM)
- **IT asset inventory & CIA scoring**: Central catalog of IT and cyber assets, with confidentiality / integrity / availability assessments and risk profiles per asset.  
- **Vulnerability & issue management**: Integrations with scanners (e.g., Tenable/Qualys) to bring vulnerabilities into the platform, link them to assets and controls, and drive workflows to remediation.  
- **Risk assessments & KRIs**: Structured IT/cyber risk assessments, risk registers specific to IT domains, and dashboards for cyber posture and trends.  
- **Cyber Risk Report** (add‑on) to synthesize technical risk data into **board‑level reporting** via Activity Center and Boards.  

#### IT Vendor Risk Management
Overlap between IT risk and third‑party risk (security focus). Key capabilities:  
- Central **IT vendor inventory**, tiering, and questionnaires.  
- Continuous monitoring of vendor security posture via external ratings/intel, SLA tracking, and alerts.  
- Risk‑based workflows for onboarding, reassessment, and remediation across cyber and compliance dimensions.  

---

### 3. Third‑Party & Vendor Risk

Diligent has two main third‑party risk families: **3rdRisk** (AI‑native TPRM) and **Third Party Manager** (compliance‑driven TPRM), plus IT‑specific vendor risk (above).

#### 3rdRisk (AI‑Native Third‑Party Risk Management)
An AI‑first TPRM platform now branded as a Diligent product.  

Capabilities:
- **End‑to‑end third‑party lifecycle**: centralized third‑party register; onboarding, tiering, assessments, document review, and continuous monitoring in one place.  
- **Multi‑domain risk coverage**: cyber, financial, operational, legal/regulatory, ESG, and reputational risk in a single model.  
- **AI Document Analyzer**: ingest contracts, audit reports, certificates, etc., extract key data, pre‑fill assessments, and suggest conclusions/next steps.  
- **AI risk profiles & country risk**: dynamic AI‑generated third‑party and country risk profiles, including sanctions, ESG, and other macro factors.  
- **AI‑supported remediation & tier discovery**: AI recommendations for remediation actions and automated suggestions of vendor tiering based on risk.  
- **Continuous monitoring & alerts** via integrations (e.g., security ratings, financial data, sanctions feeds) plus Teams/Slack/email notifications.  
- **Guided implementation** typically completed in ~10 days for initial rollout.  

#### Third Party Manager (TPM / 3PM)
More traditional TPRM for integrity, ABAC and compliance programs.  

Capabilities:
- **Lifecycle workflows**: onboarding → screening (sanctions, PEP, adverse media) → risk‑based assessment → due diligence → training/attestations → monitoring → offboarding.  
- **Risk‑based due diligence** with configurable tiers and integrated analyst‑led investigations (Diligent due diligence services).  
- **Embedded training & attestations** for third parties (ABAC, ethics, etc.).  
- **Analytics (“Insights”)**: dashboards for third‑party populations, red‑flag patterns, case status and KPIs across the TPRM program.  
- **Audit‑ready logs** and evidence to demonstrate a defensible, risk‑based approach to third‑party due diligence.  

---

### 4. How it all connects (Diligent One & Diligent AI)

Across these risk products, the **Diligent One Platform** and **Diligent AI** provide shared capabilities:  

- **Common data & identity layer**: one platform for users, org structure, and reporting across Boards, Risk, Audit, and Compliance.  
- **AI services**:  
  - AI Risk Discovery (for ERM & AI Risk Essentials).  
  - AI‑driven reporting canvases and ERM board reports (Moody’s‑powered).  
  - Analytics via Activity Center and ACL / ACL AI Studio for deeper continuous monitoring and testing.  
- **Boards integration** so risk insights and dashboards show up directly where directors work.  

This is the core capability map: **AI Risk Essentials for simple ERM**, **Diligent ERM (Risk Pro/Plus) for full enterprise risk**, **IT Risk Management for cyber/IT risk**, and **3rdRisk/Third Party Manager for third‑party risk**, all sitting on Diligent One with shared AI, analytics, and board‑level reporting.
